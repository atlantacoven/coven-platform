This guide covers everything you need to know in your first week as a member. For a higher-level walkthrough, also see the [Start Here](/start-here/) page.

## Your First Visit

1. **Join Signal** — most day-to-day communication happens in our Signal group ([contact us](/contact/) for an invite).
2. **Take the orientation tour** — covers all zones and safety equipment.
3. **Sign waivers** — liability and photo waivers must be on file.
4. **Get your fob** — received after signing your waivers; grants access.

## Access Levels

| Level     | Requirements  | Access                            |
| --------- | ------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Visitor   | Accompanied by a member | General workshop                  |
| Member    | Monthly dues  | All tools after orientation       |
| Certified | Per-tool quiz | Advanced tools (laser, CNC, etc.) |

## Tool Certifications

Some tools require a short certification before unsupervised use:

- **3D Printers** — 15-minute orientation
- **Laser Cutter** — [safety quiz](/learning/tutorials/laser-cutter-safety/) (required) + in-person training (~15 min)
- **Drill Press** — in-person training (~15 min)
- **Power Tools** — requires confirmation of general tool safety
- **Embroidery Machine** — in-person training (1 hour)
- **Soldering Station** requires confirmation of general tool safety

**Station descriptions:**

- **Member Storage** — personal storage for ongoing projects
- **Power Tool Library** — shared power tools available for checkout
- **Sewing / Cosplay Station** — sewing machines, serger, dress forms, and fabric workspace
- **Wood Workshop** — workbench, hand tools, drill press, and dust collection
- **3D Print Farm** — FDM and resin printers
- **Analogue Station** — DVD, VHS, and cassette production and encoding
- **Laser Cutter + Materials** — laser cutter with adjacent material storage (filament etc..)
- **Electronics Station 1 & 2** — soldering, bench power supplies, and component storage
- **Office Desk / Coffee Station** — admin area and refreshments
- **Central Table** — shared work surface for layout, assembly, and collaboration

## Rules of the Space

1. Clean up after yourself (5-minute rule).
2. Return tools to their designated homes, happy tools == happy home.
3. Report broken equipment immediately in Signal .
4. Guests must be accompanied by a member.
5. Know the location of the fire extinguisher, fire blanket, and other safety equipment before using the space.
6. Honor the color-coded labels on components and materials:
   - 🟢 **Green** — free to use
   - 🔵 **Blue/Purple** — free to use, but please replace after use
   - 🩷 **Pink** — ask before use
   - 🟠 **Orange** — do not use

## Getting Help

- **Signal** — tag a founder for quick questions.
- **Office hours** — available by appointment.
- **Peer tutoring** — ask questions in the Signal chat to learn from other members.

## First Project Ideas

Not sure what to make? Try one of these to get familiar with the tools:

- Custom phone stand (3D print, ~2 hours)
- Engraved keychain (laser, ~30 minutes)
- LED circuit (soldering, ~1 hour)
- Simple shelf (woodshop, ~3 hours)

## xTool P2

Our flagship laser cutter. Capable of cutting up to 12mm wood and 15mm acrylic.

### Specifications

- Laser power: 55W CO2
- Work area: 600 x 308 mm
- Max cutting depth: 18mm (wood)
- Software: xTool Creative Space

### Safety Requirements

- Must pass the Laser Safety Quiz
- Never leave the machine unattended during operation
- Exhaust must be running before starting any job

### Materials Guide

| Material     | Cut | Engrave | Notes                 |
| ------------ | --- | ------- | --------------------- |
| Baltic Birch | Yes | Yes     | Up to 12mm            |
| Acrylic      | Yes | Yes     | Up to 15mm            |
| Leather      | Yes | Yes     | Natural only          |
| Glass        | No  | Yes     | Use rotary attachment |
| PVC / Vinyl  | No  | No      | Toxic fumes           |

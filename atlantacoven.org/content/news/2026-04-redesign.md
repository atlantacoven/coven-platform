The website you're reading just got a head-to-toe overhaul. Out goes the
generic landing page; in comes a layout closer to how the space actually feels:
big photos, polaroids, ransom-note headlines, and a cream-paper palette.

## What changed

- **Homepage** is now photo-led, with a polaroid hero slideshow and a tilted
  mosaic of recent work in the studio.
- **Section pages** (charter, code of conduct, FAQ, learning, workshops, tour,
  start here, contact) all follow the same zine-flyer pattern.
- **Articles** get hand-set headlines, stamp tags, equipment chips, and a
  "back to the workshop" footer.
- **Accessibility** improvements: skip-to-content link, focus-visible outlines,
  semantic landmarks, and proper aria attributes on the mobile menu.

## What's next

- Member dashboard at `members.atlantacoven.org` (React SPA + Supabase auth)
- Stripe-backed membership signup
- Project gallery with member submissions

## Recent Updates

### April 2025 — Website Launch & Content Update
We've launched our updated website with comprehensive information about The Coven's mission, governance, and community. The site now includes:
- Complete Founders Agreement documentation
- Code of Conduct for community safety
- FAQ answering common questions
- Equipment and member information

### April 2025 — Founders Agreement Finalized
Julien, Maddie C, Maddie Y, and Sophie signed the Founders Agreement, establishing:
- Trans-focused membership mission
- Founder governance structure with voting rights
- Financial commitments and lease responsibilities
- Equipment ownership and safety policies

### March 2025 — Lease Secured
Signed lease for [Studioplex](https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/11742136#map=19/33.756108/-84.366843):
- 6-month lease starting June 1, 2025
- Month-to-month after initial term
- Lavender walls requested as landlord improvement
- Shared lounge/kitchen access in building

### Q1 2025 — Infrastructure Planning
Designed self-hosted technical infrastructure:
- Proxmox VE cluster on-premises for data sovereignty
- Self-hosted Supabase for auth, database, and storage
- Pangolin VPS for reverse proxy and SSO
- Phase 2: Member dashboard, 3D printer monitoring, access control

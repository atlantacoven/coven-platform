---
title: "Equipment Policies"
description: "How we acquire, share, maintain, and store equipment at The Coven."
---

## Acquisition & Approval

- All equipment brought into the space requires founder approval.
- Founders may store personal equipment in the space.
- Members may bring personal equipment with founder approval.
- Equipment purchased with community funds becomes property of The Coven.

## Usage & Safety

- Use of any tool requires basic orientation and owner sign-off.
- Shared equipment must include a documented crash course and owner approval before use.
- Members are responsible for cleaning up after themselves and reporting issues.
- Consumables (filament, fabric, components) start as bring-your-own. Shared purchasing systems may be implemented as the community grows.

## Maintenance & Repair

- Maintenance and repair of shared equipment is funded by community vote.
- Equipment owners are encouraged to provide basic training for shared use.
- Founders maintain authority over equipment safety and suitability for the space.

## Storage & Organization

- Founders may store equipment and personal projects.
- Members receive an assigned cubby for personal items.
- Shared tools should be returned to their designated locations after use.

## Safety First

All equipment use must comply with our [Code of Conduct](/code-of-conduct/) and safety practices. Founders retain authority to restrict or prohibit equipment that poses safety risks or undermines our mission as a queer-centered creative space.

This guide covers everything you need to know in your first week as a member. For a higher-level walkthrough, also see the [Start Here](/start-here/) page.

## Your First Visit

1. **Get your fob** — check in at the front desk to receive your 24/7 access fob.
2. **Take the orientation tour** — covers all zones and safety equipment.
3. **Join Signal** — most day-to-day communication happens in our Signal group ([contact us](/contact/) for an invite).
4. **Sign waivers** — liability and photo waivers must be on file.

## Access Levels

| Level     | Requirements  | Access                            |
| --------- | ------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Visitor   | Day pass      | General workshop                  |
| Member    | Monthly dues  | All tools after orientation       |
| Certified | Per-tool quiz | Advanced tools (laser, CNC, etc.) |

## Tool Certifications

Some tools require a short certification before unsupervised use:

- **3D Printers** — 15-minute orientation
- **Laser Cutter** — [safety quiz](/learning/tutorials/laser-cutter-safety/) (required)
- **CNC Router** — in-person training (~2 hours)
- **Welding** — external certification required

## Rules of the Space

1. Clean up after yourself (5-minute rule).
2. Return tools to their designated homes.
3. Report broken equipment immediately in Signal.
4. No sleeping in the space.
5. Guests must be accompanied by a member.

## Getting Help

- **Signal** — `#help-desk` for quick questions.
- **Office hours** — founders available Tue/Thu 6–9pm.
- **Peer tutoring** — post in `#skill-share` to learn from other members.

## First Project Ideas

Not sure what to make? Try one of these to get familiar with the tools:

- Custom phone stand (3D print, ~2 hours)
- Engraved keychain (laser, ~30 minutes)
- LED circuit (soldering, ~1 hour)
- Simple shelf (woodshop, ~3 hours)

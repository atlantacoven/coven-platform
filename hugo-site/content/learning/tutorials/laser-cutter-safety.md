## Safety First

The xTool P2 is a powerful 55W CO2 laser. It can cut and engrave wood, acrylic, leather, and more. It can also cause serious injury or start fires if misused.

## Requirements

You **must** complete this safety tutorial and pass a short quiz before using the laser cutter unsupervised.

## Hazards

| Hazard      | Prevention                                                                     |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Eye damage  | Never operate with the lid open. Always use the red-dot pointer for alignment. |
| Fire        | Never leave a cut unattended. Keep a fire extinguisher nearby.                 |
| Toxic fumes | Ensure the exhaust system is running. Never cut PVC or vinyl.                  |
| Burns       | The material and bed can be hot after cutting. Let them cool.                  |

## Prohibited Materials

**Never** cut or engrave:

- PVC, vinyl, or ABS (releases toxic chlorine gas)
- Polycarbonate (discolors and cracks)
- Fiberglass or carbon fiber
- Metals (the P2 cannot cut metal)
- Unknown plastics

## Pre-Cut Checklist

1. [ ] Exhaust fan is on and venting outside
2. [ ] Material is flat and secured
3. [ ] Focus is set correctly for material thickness
4. [ ] Job preview looks correct in xTool Creative Space
5. [ ] You will stay with the machine for the entire job
6. [ ] Fire extinguisher is accessible

## Emergency Procedures

If a fire starts:

1. Lift the lid immediately (this stops the laser beam)
2. Use the CO2 fire extinguisher
3. Do not open the main exhaust (can feed oxygen to the fire)
4. Notify a staff member

## Quiz

After reading this guide, take the safety quiz at the front desk. You need 8/10 to pass.
